Why become a member?


Accidents are unavoidable, but now high out of pocket repairs can be.

Over 6 million car accidents occur each year in the U.S. alone. Most people are unprepared financially for the burdens of high deductible costs before their insurance provider begins contributions. We provide our members financial security and peace of mind by covering deductible costs with respect to their membership status.
