Why become a member?


As automotive insurance prices continue to rise, we are here to offer additional financial options to alleviate out-of-pocket costs.

Most people are unprepared financially for the burdens of high deductible costs before their insurance provider begins contributions. We provide our members financial security and peace of mind by offering up to an average 80% savings on out-of-pocket repair expenses.

We are reshaping the auto industry, and want to take you along for the ride!

48 Hour Response Time
We process claims within 48 hours of receipt. This ensures our members get back on the road sooner and back to the things that matter most.
Direct Payment
Once a claim is processed through your insurance, we will accept the deductible charges based on your membership plan. All payments will be directly dispersed to your auto-body shop, creating a seamless experience that ensures you’re up and running the moment the repairs are completed.
Easy Online Portal
Manage your account and services through our easy to use online portal. Our member portal is your one-stop-shop for all your membership needs. View real time updates on your vehicle claim, change personal and payment information, add more vehicles to your account, and speak with a customer service representative. All this, and much more!
