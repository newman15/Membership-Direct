The Gold Membership plan provides payment for out of pocket deductibles totaling $1000 or up to 3 claims per year, whichever comes first. When an accident occurs and auto-body repairs are required, Membership Direct will provide payment directly to the customer’s auto-body repair shop of choice. Membership Direct works as an additional service to existing car insurance providers; therefor it is required that an existing claim be filed and approved through the member’s insurance provider before receiving services through Membership Direct.


It is the customer’s responsibility to report all accidents to their insurance providers and Membership Direct before receiving any services outlined in their membership. Membership Direct will not provide payment for any services if a claim has not been filed and approved through their car insurance provider prior to requesting coverage for their out of pocket deductibles. Once a claim has been successfully processed, through their insurance providers, members will utilize their online portal to submit their claims and request for payment through Membership Direct. Step by step we will guide you through the required documents and provide updates, via email, as your claim is processed and payments dispersed to the appropriate auto shop.


Membership services, in the form of deductible payments, will be provided directly to the auto-body shop specified in the customer’s claim. Members may check their online portal at any time to track the progress as payments are processed and dispersed. In addition, members can opt for email updates that will notify them of any changes or if additional documents or information is needed to process a claim.
